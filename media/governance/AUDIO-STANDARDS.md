# AUDIO QUALITY & STUDIO CALIBRATION STEPS

Purpose: Ensure nervous-system safety through technical audio clarity and softness.

## 1. Voice Calibration
- Close-mic, warm, and conversational.
- EQ target: Reduce harsh highs (3k-4kHz low-pass); gentle boost in low-mids (200Hz-400Hz) for warmth.
- Processing: Minimal denoising and gentle compression for "closeness" feel.

## 2. Ambience Calibration
- Rain Layer: Soft window rain only. Reduce volume by -15dB to -20dB relative to voice.
- Filtering: Remove high-frequency hiss and sharpness. Soft, distant texture.
- Space: Not emotionally immersive; feel signal should be grounded in a safe indoor room.

## 3. Music Calibration
- Piano: Minimal, secondary, and distant. Reduce volume by -20dj to -25dB.
- Priority: Silence > Music. If piano shapes emotion too adgressively, pull it back.

## 4. Mastering & Pacing
- Dynamic Range: Natural, not loudness-compressed.
- Pauses: Increase breathing space. The nervous system relaxes during the spaces.
- Final Render: Clean, free of AI artifacts, hiss, or synthetic harshness.
