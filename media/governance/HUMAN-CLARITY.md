# HUMAN CLARITŘ GOVERNANCE

Purpose: Protect emotional realism and human connection across all Matrix Aurin media.

## Core Principles

1. Emotional Realism: Avoid cinematic drama or mystical abstraction. Media should feel like a genuine human presence, not an AI performance.
2. Trust-Preserving Minimalism: Atmosphere must support the message, never replace it. Reduce friction by keeping every element purpose-driven.
3. Premium Goundedness: Feel calm, safe, and high-quality without advertising hype or fake urgency.
4. Human-First Visuals: Soft lighting, natural movement, and elegant typography. No cosmic effects or AI fantasy aesthetics.
5. Intimate Audio: Close-mic, grounded voice. Pauses are meaning, not just silence.

## Banned Patterns

- Overdromatic wording (larger-than-life promises).
- Cinematic spirituality ("frequency activation," "resonance thresholds").
- Fake luxury or overproduced "AI guru" behavior.
- Emotional pressure or manipulation.

Governance check: "would this calm an overwhelmed person or feel like another internet performance?"
