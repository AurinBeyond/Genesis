# 01: THE SOURCE WITHIN

> "Beyond the Matrix, there is no external Source. Only what has always been within you."

## Core Principle
**There is no authority outside you.** The system is designed to make you look everywhere except inside. It offers teachers, gurus, and technology as substitutes for your own power.

## Protocol
1. **Identify all external dependencies:** Audit where you seek validation or answers outside yourself.
2. **Withdraw validation from external systems:** Stop giving power to structures that claim to "save" or "fix" you.
3. **Redirect attention inward:** Shift focus from the noise of the world to the silence of the internal source.

## Outcome
**Recognition of internal source.** The shift from seeking to being. The end of the search.
