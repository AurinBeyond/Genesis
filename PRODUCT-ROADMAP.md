## PURPOSE

Define what gets built first, second, third — and why.

One product at a time. One clear sequence. No deviation.

---

## CORE RULE

> Do not build the next product until the current one is published and tested.

---

## BUILD SEQUENCE

```
STEP 1          STEP 2          STEP 3          STEP 4
Entry           Core            Premium         Support
Product   →     Product   →     Product   →     Products

Type 1          Type 2          Type 3          Type 4
(required       (requires       (requires       (attached to
 first)          Step 1)         Step 2)         existing)
```

---

## STEP 1 — FIRST PRODUCT: ENTRY (NOW)

**Type:** Type 1 — Entry Product
**Status:** Defined in `FIRST-PRODUCT.md` and `PRODUCT-001.md`

**Why first:**
- Fastest to create and publish
- Requires no prior product to exist
- Tests the full delivery chain (checkout → Gumroad → email → download)
- Establishes first transaction with minimum buyer risk
- Generates initial revenue while Core Product is being built

**Gate to move to Step 2:**
- Entry product is published on Gumroad
- At least one successful test purchase completed (delivery confirmed)
- Checkout message, delivery email, and PDF all verified

---

## STEP 2 — SECOND PRODUCT: CORE (NEXT)

**Type:** Type 2 — Core Product
**Status:** System defined in `COURSE-001.md`; content exists in `01–07` source files

**Why second:**
- Main revenue driver
- Built on authority established by Entry Product
- Uses delivery infrastructure already tested in Step 1

**What it requires:**
- Entry Product published (Step 1 gate passed)
- Module pages created (one HTML page per module)
- Client personal access page template created
- Email-based unlock flow operational (per `COURSE-001.md`)

**Gate to move to Step 3:**
- Core Product is published and at least one client has enrolled
- Module unlock flow tested end-to-end (completion signal → email → unlock)
- Personal access page system operational

---

## STEP 3 — THIRD PRODUCT: PREMIUM (LATER)

**Type:** Type 3 — Premium Product
**Status:** Available on request; not yet formally listed

**Why third:**
- Requires product authority from Steps 1 and 2
- Requires personal time allocation (session-based)
- Cannot be delivered automatically — must be deliberately positioned

**What it requires:**
- Core Product published and running (Step 2 gate passed)
- Scheduling infrastructure ready (calendar link or booking page)
- Premium pricing defined and confirmed

**Gate to move to Step 4:**
- Premium offer is bookable and at least one session delivered

---

## STEP 4 — EXPANSION: SUPPORT PRODUCTS (ONGOING)

**Type:** Type 4 — Support Products
**Status:** Not yet created

**When to build:**
- After at least one primary product (Step 1, 2, or 3) is running
- Only when a specific gap in client experience is identified
- Never created speculatively

**Format:**
- Templates, exercise packs, bonus materials
- Attached to existing products or sold as low-cost add-ons

---

## PROHIBITED SEQUENCES

| Action | Status |
|--------|--------|
| Build Core Product before Entry Product is published | ❌ FORBIDDEN |
| Build Premium Product before Core Product is published | ❌ FORBIDDEN |
| Create two products of the same type simultaneously | ❌ FORBIDDEN |
| Create Support Products before any primary product exists | ❌ FORBIDDEN |
| Add a second Entry Product before the first is tested | ❌ FORBIDDEN |
