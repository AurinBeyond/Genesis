## PURPOSE

Define the desire and intrigue layer of the traffic system.

The entry message does not sell. It creates contact.

```
RECOGNITION → INTRIGUE → PAUSE
```

---

## DESIRE LAYER DEFINITION

Three elements are active at every entry point.

---

### 1. RECOGNITION

Speak to what the user already feels before they have words for it.

Do not describe the product. Name the internal state.

> The user does not arrive empty. They arrive carrying something they have not yet named.
> The entry message names it for them — without explaining it away.

**Function:** The user feels seen. Not sold to.

---

### 2. INTRIGUE

Leave a gap. Do not complete the thought.

The entry message presents a direction, not a destination.

> Do not explain everything.
> Create an unfinished loop — something the mind wants to close.

**Function:** Curiosity activates forward movement. The user moves toward the gap.

---

### 3. EMOTIONAL CONTACT

Calm. Direct. Personal.

No pressure. No hype. No performance.

> The tone itself is the message.
> A message that does not push makes the user lean in.

**Function:** Trust forms before a single claim is made.

---

## ENTRY MESSAGE

*Applied to: `index.html` / social content / first contact point*

---

### STRUCTURE

```
RECOGNITION — name what they already feel
INTRIGUE    — open a gap, do not close it
PAUSE       — one line that holds
```

---

### ENTRY MESSAGE COPY

**Line 1 — Recognition**

> You already know something is off.

---

**Line 2 — Intrigue**

> Most people spend their entire lives solving the wrong problem.

---

**Line 3 — Pause**

> The source you've been looking for is not outside.

---

### HOW TO READ THESE THREE LINES

| Line | Function | What it does NOT do |
|------|----------|---------------------|
| You already know something is off. | Names internal state — validates what they feel | Does not explain what is "off" — leaves the gap open |
| Most people spend their entire lives solving the wrong problem. | Creates the unfinished loop | Does not tell them what the right problem is |
| The source you've been looking for is not outside. | Single internal trigger — the pivot | Does not resolve the idea — it opens inward |

The message ends. No call to action follows immediately.

The silence after the pause is part of the structure.

## EXTENDED ENTRY VARIATIONS

For use in social content, bio lines, or story captions.

Each variation follows the same 3-step pattern.

---

**Variation A**

> Everything you need to find is already present.

---

**Variation B**

> You have been taught to seek.
> Nobody taught you to stop.
> That moment — when the search ends — is the beginning.

---

**Variation C**

> That recognition is not coincidence.
> It is where you start.

## RULES FOR APPLICATION

| Rule | Instruction |
|------|------------|
| No exaggeration | Every line must be true — not amplified |
| No fake mystery | The gap is real, not manufactured |
| No manipulation | Recognition must be genuine, not engineered to trigger |
| No explanation | The entry message opens — it does not resolve |
| No CTA in the pause line | The pause stands alone. What follows is separate. |
