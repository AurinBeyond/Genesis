# DELIVERY & ACCESS POLICY v1.0

**Brand:** Pure Soul Life
**Scope:** All products, services, and digital and physical content
**Status:** Active

---

## I. DELIVERY TYPES

Products and services are delivered in two formats:

---

### 1. DIGITAL DELIVERY

Digital products, services, and information are delivered electronically via:

- email
- phone (SMS / messaging apps)
- access links

---

### 2. PHYSICAL PRODUCT (BOOKS)

Physical books are not sold directly through this platform.

Clients are provided with official purchase links to external platforms (e.g., Amazon), where the book can be ordered.

---

## II. DELIVERY METHOD

| Product Type | Delivery Method |
|-------------|----------------|
| Digital content (guides, modules, PDFs) | Sent to client's email or phone |
| Session confirmations | Delivered after booking confirmation |
| Physical books | External purchase link (e.g., Amazon) provided on page, confirmation, or email |

---

## III. DELIVERY TIMING

| Product Type | Timing |
|-------------|--------|
| Digital products | Immediately after successful payment |
| Session confirmations | After booking is completed |
| External product links (books) | Immediately — on page, confirmation, or email |

---

## IV. EXTERNAL PLATFORM RESPONSIBILITY

For physical book purchases:

- All transactions are handled by the external platform (e.g., Amazon)
- Delivery, shipping, returns, and customer service are the responsibility of that platform

Pure Soul Life is not responsible for:

- shipping delays
- product availability
- external payment processing
- order fulfillment issues

Any issues with a physical book purchase must be directed to the relevant external platform.

---

## V. CLIENT RESPONSIBILITY

The client is responsible for:

- providing correct contact details at the time of purchase
- checking their email and/or phone for messages
- following external purchase links correctly

Pure Soul Life cannot be held responsible for non-delivery caused by incorrect contact details provided by the client.

---

## VI. ACCESS RESPONSIBILITY

To receive and access digital products, the client must ensure:

- access to the email or phone number used at checkout
- ability to receive messages from external senders
- checking spam and junk folders if expected content is not found in the main inbox

---

## VII. DELIVERY CONFIRMATION

After a successful purchase, the client receives:

- confirmation of purchase
- next steps
- access details, download links, or external purchase links

This confirmation is delivered to the email address provided at checkout.

---

## VIII. TECHNICAL LIMITATIONS

Delivery may be affected by:

- email provider delays
- spam filters blocking automated messages
- network issues on the client side

Pure Soul Life is not responsible for delays caused by third-party systems or the client's own technical environment.

---

## IX. SUPPORT

**Digital products:**
If digital delivery is not received within 15 minutes of purchase, the client must contact support:

- Email: `contact.puresoul@proton.me`
- Include: order ID and the email address used at checkout

**Physical products:**
Support for physical book orders must be requested directly from the external platform (e.g., Amazon). Pure Soul Life cannot intervene in external platform orders.

---

## FINAL STATEMENT

Digital products and services are delivered electronically to the contact details provided by the client.

Physical books are purchased through external platforms via provided links.

Responsibility is distributed accordingly between Pure Soul Life and the relevant external provider.
