# PRICING & CURRENCY POLICY v1.0

**Brand:** Pure Soul Life
**Scope:** All products, services, and transactions

---

## I. BASE CURRENCY

All prices are defined and processed in Norwegian Kroner (NOK).

---

## II. DISPLAYED PRICES

For international customers, prices may be displayed in local currency for convenience.

These converted values are estimates based on available exchange rate data at the time of viewing.

---

## III. FINAL PAYMENT AMOUNT

All transactions are processed in NOK.

The final amount charged may vary slightly from the displayed converted value due to:

- Real-time exchange rate fluctuations
- Payment provider conversion rates
- Bank-specific fees or adjustments

---

## IV. THIRD-PARTY FEES

Additional fees may be applied by the client's bank or payment provider, including:

- Currency conversion fees
- International transaction fees

Pure Soul Life does not control and is not responsible for such charges.

---

## V. CLIENT ACKNOWLEDGEMENT

By completing a purchase, the client confirms that:

- They understand that the base currency is NOK.
- They accept possible differences due to currency conversion.
- They are responsible for any additional fees imposed by their payment provider.

---

## VI. TRANSPARENCY PRINCIPLE

Pure Soul Life aims to provide clear and fair pricing at all times.

If clarification is needed, the client is encouraged to contact support before completing a purchase.

---

## FINAL STATEMENT

All pricing is defined in NOK.

Displayed conversions are provided for guidance only and do not represent the exact final charged amount.
