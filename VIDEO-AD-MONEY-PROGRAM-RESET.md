## VISUAL STYLE

* **Framing:** Extreme close-up throughout — face, eyes, hands only. No wide establishing shots. The world outside the face does not exist.
* **Light progression:** Cold blue (screen glow) → darkness (phone down) → warm gold (right side entry). This is the full visual arc.
* **Cinematography:** Slow. Held. No camera movement except possible slow push in Scene 5.
* **Color:** Near-black with single light source. No ambient fill. High contrast.
* **Tone:** Cinematic, not documentary. Not stock. Not TikTok.
* **No emojis, no overlays, no effects beyond the light shift.**

---

## VISUAL REFERENCE — BRAND AESTHETIC

The visual direction is established by the AurinBeyond brand imagery:

* Dark navy / near-black backgrounds
* Gold as the only accent — used for light, not decoration
* Human figures in silhouette or close-up — never generic or stock
* The internal emotional state is the subject — not the external event
* Sacred geometry and structural elements are subtext, not foreground

For this video specifically:
* The "program" is shown through the face — the light from the phone, the micro-expressions, the loop
* The "reset" is shown through the light shift and the tear — not through a transformation montage
* The concept is internal. The viewer must feel it, not be told it.
