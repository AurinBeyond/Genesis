## SINGLE DELIVERY PATH

```
PAYMENT CONFIRMED → GUMROAD PROCESSES → EMAIL SENT → CLIENT OPENS → ACCESS GRANTED
```

---

## I. DELIVERY MECHANISM

All products are delivered digitally.

**Platform:** Gumroad (`aurinbeyond.gumroad.com`)

Gumroad handles:
- payment confirmation
- email dispatch
- file or content hosting
- access link generation

Pure Soul Life does not manage a separate delivery server or CRM.

---

## II. WHAT IS DELIVERED

| Product Type | Delivery Format | Delivery Time |
|-------------|----------------|---------------|
| Digital content (PDF, module, guide) | Download link in email | Immediate |
| Academy access | Access link in email | Immediate |
| 1:1 session booking | Booking link or calendar invite | Within 24 hours of payment |

All delivery is non-physical. No shipping occurs.

---

## III. DELIVERY SEQUENCE

**Step 1 — Payment confirmed**
Gumroad receives successful payment. Order ID is generated.

**Step 2 — Confirmation email sent**
Gumroad automatically sends an email to the buyer's address containing:
- Order ID
- Product name
- Amount charged (in NOK)
- Access link or download link

**Step 3 — Client opens email**
Client receives the email. They click the provided link.

**Step 4 — Access granted**
Client reaches the content. No additional steps required.

---

## IV. DELIVERY FAILURE PROTOCOL

If client does not receive the email:

1. Client checks spam/junk folder
2. Client waits up to 15 minutes
3. If still not received → client contacts support at `contact.puresoul@proton.me` with order ID
4. Support verifies the purchase in Gumroad dashboard
5. Support manually resends access or provides direct link

**Resolution target:** within 24 hours of contact.

---

## V. ACCESS DURATION

- Access to purchased content is permanent unless product terms specify otherwise
- If a product is discontinued, clients who have purchased it are notified in advance
- Pure Soul Life may update content within the same product at any time

---

## VI. TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS (CLIENT SIDE)

- Stable internet connection
- Working email account (used at checkout)
- Device capable of opening PDF, video, or web-based content

Technical issues on the client side are the client's responsibility (per `client-agreement.html`).
