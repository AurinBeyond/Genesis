## COMMUNICATION PRINCIPLE

Pure Soul Life communicates only when necessary.

```
NO unsolicited messages
NO automated sequences
NO cold outreach
```

Communication is triggered by:
- a purchase event (automated by Gumroad)
- a client message (manual response)

---

## I. COMMUNICATION TOUCHPOINTS

### Touchpoint 1 — Order Confirmation Email (Automated)

**Trigger:** Successful payment on Gumroad

**Sent by:** Gumroad

**Contains:**
- Order ID
- Product name
- Amount charged (NOK)
- Access or download link
- Gumroad support reference

**Action required from Pure Soul Life:** none

---

### Touchpoint 2 — Success Page (`success.html`)

**Trigger:** Client redirected after payment

**Contains:**
- Confirmation of purchase activation
- Instructions to check email
- Support contact reference

**Tone:** Direct. No celebration theater.

---

### Touchpoint 3 — Support Response (Manual)

**Trigger:** Client sends email to `contact.puresoul@proton.me`

**Response time:** Within 24 hours

**Scope:**
- Delivery issues (email not received)
- Access problems
- Questions about the product
- Refund requests

**Rules:**
- Respond in the language the client used
- Keep response short and clear
- Do not promise outcomes or results
- Do not offer unsolicited advice
- If the issue cannot be resolved directly, escalate to HUMAN review

---

### Touchpoint 4 — 1:1 Session Confirmation (Manual)

**Trigger:** Client purchases or books a 1:1 session

**Action:** Pure Soul Life sends a calendar link or scheduling confirmation within 24 hours

**Contains:**
- Date and time confirmation or link to select time
- Format (video call platform)
- Preparation instructions if applicable

---

## II. TONE STANDARD

All client-facing communication must be:

- Direct
- Neutral
- Clear
- Free of pressure or urgency tactics

Do NOT use:
- "Amazing opportunity" language
- Urgency that is not real
- Emotional manipulation
- Vague promises

---

## III. WHAT PURE SOUL LIFE DOES NOT DO

- Send marketing emails without explicit client consent
- Follow up on abandoned checkouts
- Send unsolicited product recommendations
- Contact clients on social media about purchases

---

## IV. SUPPORT CONTACT

**Email:** `contact.puresoul@proton.me`

This is the single support channel.

All support communication flows through this address.

---

## V. REFUND COMMUNICATION

If a client requests a refund:

1. Support acknowledges the request within 48 hours
2. Request is reviewed against the cancellation/refund policy in `client-agreement.html`
3. Decision is communicated in writing
4. If approved, refund is processed via Gumroad

No refund commitments are made outside of the stated policy.
