# SOCIAL MEDIA COMPLIANCE
# Status: ACTIVE — GLOBAL RULESET

---

## PURPOSE

This document defines all external communication safety rules.

It ensures:

- platform compliance (TikTok, Instagram, etc.)
- legal safety
- brand consistency
- risk prevention

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE

All content must be:

- observational
- interpretative
- non-absolute
- non-clinical

---

## ABSOLUTE PROHIBITIONS

Content must NOT:

- make guarantees
- promise results
- claim transformation certainty
- present outcomes as universal truth

Forbidden examples:

❌ "This will change your life"  
❌ "You will break all patterns"  
❌ "This guarantees results"  

---

## MEDICAL & PSYCHOLOGICAL LANGUAGE BAN

Strictly NOT allowed:

- medical terminology
- psychological diagnosis terms
- clinical language
- therapy framing
- treatment claims

Forbidden:

- "trauma healing"
- "anxiety disorder"
- "depression"
- "PTSD"
- "nervous system regulation"
- "therapy process"
- "clinical improvement"

---

## SAFE LANGUAGE SYSTEM

Replace with:

- patterns
- reactions
- responses
- states
- experiences

Examples:

❌ "This helps with anxiety"  
✅ "This may change how you respond in certain situations"

❌ "You have trauma"  
✅ "You may be repeating certain patterns"

---

## AUTHORITY LIMITATION

Content must NEVER present itself as:

- medical advice
- psychological treatment
- therapy alternative

Content must remain:

- informational
- reflective
- exploratory

---

## SOCIAL PLATFORM SAFETY

Avoid:

- extreme claims
- absolute statements
- sensitive keywords in direct form
- aggressive persuasion

Use:

- soft framing
- neutral tone
- indirect phrasing when needed

---

## SALES COMPLIANCE

Allowed:

- soft invitation
- optional next step
- neutral suggestion

Forbidden:

- pressure tactics
- urgency manipulation
- emotional forcing

Example:

❌ "Buy now or miss this"  
✅ "If you want to explore this deeper, you can continue here"

---

## CONTENT STRUCTURE SAFETY

Each content piece must:

1. present observation  
2. allow interpretation  
3. leave space for choice  

Content must NOT:

- push conclusion
- force belief

---

## FLEXIBLE TOPIC RULE (CRITICAL)

Topics are NOT fixed.

Agents are allowed to:

- create new topics
- modify angles
- adapt themes

Agents are NOT allowed to:

- break compliance rules
- introduce prohibited language
- create unsafe claims

---

## RESPONSIBILITY

All agents must:

- validate output against this file
- reject unsafe content
- adjust phrasing when needed

---

## FAILURE CONDITION

Reject content if:

- contains absolute claims
- uses clinical language
- creates pressure
- implies guaranteed outcome

---

## SUCCESS CONDITION

- content is safe
- content is clear
- content is compliant
- content is scalable across platforms
