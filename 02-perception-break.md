# 02: PERCEPTION BREAK

> "What you see is not what is — it is what you were trained to see."

## Core Principle

**Perception is conditioned, not objective.**
What you experience as reality is filtered through learned patterns, beliefs, and automatic interpretation.

To see clearly, the filter must be recognized.

---

## Protocol

1. **Observe automatic reactions**
   Notice the immediate emotional or intellectual label your mind assigns to a situation.

2. **Interrupt interpretation**
   Pause before the story completes.
   Ask: *Is this observation — or conditioning?*

3. **Separate perception from reality**
   Identify what actually happened before meaning is added.
   Distinguish raw input from interpretation.

---

## Outcome

**Awareness of distortion.**
Recognition that your reactions are not to reality, but to your perception of it.
